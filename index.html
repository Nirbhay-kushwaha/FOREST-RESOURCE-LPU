Natural Rescources

Natural resources are the raw materials and substances that are found in the environment and are utilized by humans for various purposes. These resources are essential for the survival and development of civilizations, playing a crucial role in supporting human life and economic activities. Natural resources can be broadly categorized into renewable and non-renewable resources.
